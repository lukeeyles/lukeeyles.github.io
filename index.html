About Me

Hi, I'm Luke, and I'm based in Sydney. I have experience in robotics, automation, and research, having recently worked on a research project involving communication between underwater robots in collaboration with Thales.

My interest in robotics combines my enjoyment in learning how things work, and my fascination with problem solving. When I’m presented with a complex problem, I find it satisfying to break the problem down into pieces, solve them individually, and combine them together into a complete project.

My Experience

Currently, I'm working as a Technology Consulting Intern at Isle Utilities, where I’m involved in discovering common problems between water utilities, and researching to find technology to solve these problems.

Previously I’ve worked as a Research and Development Intern at Jenkins Engineering Defence Systems, focusing on signal processing in MATLAB and experimental design.

I've also worked as an Assistant Lab Tutor for Introduction to Electrical Engineering at the University of Technology Sydney, where I helped students debug their electrical circuits and explained electrical engineering concepts to them.
